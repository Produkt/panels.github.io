The best comic reader for iOS

Give your comics some love.

Visually gorgeous

Panels' interface is elegant and fits perfectly in the iOS evosystem. Choose between 7 different themes, and assign different ones for light and dark modes. The main library is highly customizable: visualize your comics as grid or list in various sizes. You can also choose between many different readers to maximize your reading experience.

Features

Panels offers incredible features to enjoy all your comics in your iOS devices.

Organise your library

Use collections to easily organise all your titles.

Import documents from everywhere

Panels can import files from iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, AirDrop, iTunes, etc.

Panels Guided View

Panels uses computer vision to analize your comics' pages, so you can enjoy a panel-by-panel navigation in your own comics.

Pick up were you left off

If you use panel in more than one device, Panels will automatically sync your progress accross them.
